About Nenya Health

我们是谁 · Who We Are

嫩芽健康（Nenya Health）是一家连接香港与深圳的跨境健康管理与就医管家服务机构。我们不替代医院诊疗，而是作为医院—患者—家庭之间的第三方健康管家与决策助手，依托专业医生顾问与健康管理团队，为香港居民及中高端家庭提供从就医导航、陪诊协同、体检管理、报告解读到长期随访的全链路服务。我们的目标是让跨境就医更省心、更可控，让每一次检查与就诊都沉淀为可持续的健康管理方案。 We are a cross-border health concierge connecting Shenzhen and Hong Kong. Nenya Health does not replace clinical care; we act as a third-party steward between hospitals, patients, and families. A physician advisory board and bilingual health managers coordinate navigation, escort, check-ups, report interpretation, and longitudinal follow-up. We turn each visit into a durable health asset.

我们的定位 Mission

第三方健康管家

站在客户一侧协调医生、医院与家属，保持信息透明。Stand on the client side to coordinate clinicians, hospitals, and family with transparency.

跨境连接员

熟悉深港制度、预约逻辑与文化差异，提前规划节奏。Fluent in HK & SZ systems, schedules, and nuances for calm cadence planning.

长期陪伴者

把一次次检查转化为连续的档案、提醒与复盘。Transform every encounter into living archives, reminders, and reviews.

我们怎么做 · Methodology

四个模块 + 服务边界。Four modules plus clear service boundaries.

Intake 需求评估

访谈、资料核实、风险提醒、优先级排序。Interviews, record validation, risk alerts, and priority mapping.

Match 匹配

医院 / 科室 / 医生筛选，多方案比对与预期管理。Select hospitals, departments, and doctors with expectation management.

Deliver 交付

陪诊、体检、取报告、家属同步、医患沟通支援。Escort visits, check-ups, report pick-up, and mediate communications.

Follow 复盘

报告解读、档案沉淀、随访提醒、下一步任务。Interpretation, archiving, follow-up reminders, and next tasks.

服务边界 Service Boundaries

  • 不替代医生诊断或治疗；所有临床决策由医生与客户共同确认。We never replace licensed physicians; decisions are made jointly with them.
  • 不承诺疗效，不提供监管外医疗建议。No outcome guarantees or off-regulation advice.
  • 紧急或生命威胁情况请立刻前往急症室 / 拨打 999 或 120。In emergencies, head to A&E or call 999 / 120 immediately.

我们服务谁 · Who We Serve

港人中产 / 高净值客户 Affluent HK clients

  • 遇到跨境就医、专家 second opinion。Facing cross-border visits or second opinions.
  • 希望流程有序且保密。Need orderly, discreet flow.

港人长者 / 慢病患者 Seniors & chronic cases

  • 需要陪诊协同与家庭随访提醒。Require escort coordination and family follow-up alerts.
  • 需兼顾深港双地的复诊节奏。Need cadence across both cities.

深港中高端家庭 Dual-city families

  • 2–4 人家庭年度规划。Annual programs for 2–4 members.
  • 以家庭医生视角统筹资料。Desire family doctor view of records.
