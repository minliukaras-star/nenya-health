我们如何工作｜How We Work

方法论来自跨境家庭医生实践，4 个模块串联需求评估→资源匹配→现场交付→报告解读与随访。 Four modular stages from intake to follow-up, optimized for Shenzhen & Hong Kong realities.

需求评估 Intake

深度访谈与资料整理，定义目标、限制因素及风险提示。 Deep intake interviews, data verification, and clarity on objectives, constraints, and risks.

匹配 Match

根据病种与期望匹配深港医院 / 专科 / 专家团队，设计路径与时间表。 Map the right HK / SZ hospitals, departments, and specialists, then design cadence and timeline.

交付 Deliver

就医陪诊、体检陪同、在场协调、家属同步、差异对齐。 Escort care, check-up companionship, onsite coordination, and family communications.

复盘 Follow

报告解读、提问单、年度健康档案、随访提醒，沉淀可追踪数据。 Report interpretation, question lists, annual dossiers, and follow-up reminders with trackable data.
